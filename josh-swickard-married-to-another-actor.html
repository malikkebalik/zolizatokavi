Josh Swickard - Married to Another Actor!

Finding love is the hardest thing, right? But, this became an easy thing for Josh Swickard, the American model and actor who got more than fortunate to find the love of his life while working on a movie titled Roped.

The couple is together from one-and-a-half-year, sharing unconditional love. Wanna know more about their love life? Then let’s find out!!!

Blissful Married Life

Josh Swickard is happily married to his long-term girlfriend, Lorynn York. The pair walked down the aisle in July of 2019. The wedding was attended by both families of the bride and the groom.

Here you can see the bride looking gorgeous in the white silk gown, whereas the groom is looking stunning in his black tuxedo with a white shirt and black bow tie.

The couple flew to France to celebrate their wedding and also their honeymoon. Let’s congratulate the couple on their newly wedded life. Now, let’s see how the pair first met?

Josh and Lorynn’s First Meeting; How Things Started?

Josh met the love of his life after he signed for the movie Roped. The actor saw Lorynn at the set of the film where he instantly fell in love with her. He also explained it was love at first sight for him.

After seeing Lorynn, Josh called his father and explained about Lorynn, saying, “She is the One.” And as per his father’s advice, Josh waited for a whole year to know more about his future wife.

Eventually, the duo started dating, and they also began to appear together in various places.

Proposed His Soulmate Traditionally

Josh organized a family holiday vacation in Beaver Creek, which included the family of Lorynn. Both of the families were having a great time together while skiing at Beaver Creek.

In an interview, Josh explained that York’s family had a tradition of playing Charades. So then, he decided to propose her with a surprise while playing the game in December of 2018.

Just after proposing Loryn, Josh uploaded a picture where Loryn is flaunting her diamond ring. Additionally, Bee Shaffer also dated with her beau for a long time before sharing the wedding vows.
