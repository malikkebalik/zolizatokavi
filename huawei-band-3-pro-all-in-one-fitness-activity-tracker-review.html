HUAWEI Band 3 Pro All-in-One Fitness Activity Tracker Review

If you are struggling to stay fit, then you can get a fitness tracker to help you. A fitness tracker is a device that will constantly keep track of your activities and help you to keep going. It is a simple device that has lots of interesting features to help you achieve your fitness goal. With this device, you will be able to track your daily activities, sleep, heart rate, and more. You will be able to get smartphone functions as well. Among the various fitness trackers available in the market, the Huawei Band 3 Pro is one of the best budget-friendly devices. If you don’t want to spend too much money on fitness trackers, then this can be your best choice. Here, we are going to review this activity tracker in detail.

Design

The Huawei Band 3 Pro tracker has a very stylish design that you will instantly like. It is found in 3 different colors including black, white, dark blue, and gold. It is a rubber band encasing a small unit that has a heart rate sensor and charging ports on the back, and a touchscreen on the front. It has an AMOLED touch display and you will be able to read data even in bright sunlight. The screen is colored and you can adjust the screen brightness. It has a full touch screen and there is a home button control as well. The strap is flexible and is made of silicone. It has diagonal lines on the outside for extra texture. The strap of the band is replaceable.

Build Quality

It is a fully-featured activity band that you get for a cheap price. It has a color OLED screen, part-metal case and glass screen cover for protection. This also makes it less susceptible to scrapes and scratches. It has metal surround and the band is made of silicone. It has a plate of brushed aluminum on the top. It has a high gloss UV coating body to protect it from the harmful rays of the sun.

It has multi-sports mode, so you can track indoor/outdoor run, cycle, walk, swim, and various types of exercises. It provides a 24/7 heart rate monitor that provides accurate real-time heart rate data. It gives discrete heart rate data for every second of your activity. You can also track your sleep using this device. It can identify 6 common sleep-related issues and provide advice on having better sleep. Unlike many trackers in the market that use connected GPS, this tracker has a built-in GPS. So, you will be able to track your activities better as you will know the distance, pace, and route of your workouts.

It has 50M water resistance, so you will be able to use it in the swimming pool or shower. It has a 6-axis Imu sensor that gives stats for stroke style detection, laps, distance, calories, and more. It has different smartphone features, like receiving call and text notifications, alarm, and more. You can use it as a camera shutter remote for your phone and take better selfies and group photos.

You can use the Huawei Health app and more feedback about your fitness and health. It uses very little energy and so the battery life is long. If the sleep monitor and 24/7 heart monitor is switched off, then you can use the tracker for 12 days without charging it.

Size

It has dimensions 1.8 X 0.4 X 0.8 inches and weighs 0.64 ounces. As it’s slim and lightweight, it feels very comfortable to wear. It has one size that will fit all. The strap is watch-style with 17 fit gradations. The holes also function as ventilation. The holes are present up the strap as far as possible so that it can fit even the smallest wrists.

Comfort

It is lightweight and is very easy to put on. You can wear it comfortably all day and night. The silicone rubber band won’t irritate your wrists even when you sweat.

Effectiveness

You will be able to become a better runner with the help of this device as you will get a real-time evaluation of training effects. You can swim better as well as you will get intelligent feedback to identify and improve your strokes. It is 50M water-resistant and provides swimming data like the number of turns calories, etc. so that you can improve your swimming.

You will be able to use this tracker as a camera shutter remote. So, you won’t need to carry an extra remote to take long-distance shots. You will be able to take better group photos and selfies as well. One interesting feature of this tracker is that it can find your phone. It can trigger the ringer of the phone even if it is in the silent mode and help you to locate your phone.

Price

You can buy this fitness tracker for only $69.99. If you consider all the features it provides including sleep monitor, heart rate monitor, GPS, etc. the cost should be more than $100. You can also get it at a cheaper rate at Amazon. Sometimes, you can get it for $54.99 but the price can change at any time.

Pros

  • The clasp is secure and it feels comfortable on the wrist.
  • It has a heart rate sensor that can monitor your heart rate 24/7 and give accurate data.
  • It is a good sleep tracker and can identify whether you have any sleep-related issues or not.
  • It has long battery life.
  • It can track different types of sports and give you useful stats.
  • It has built-in GPS that will provide the distance, pace, and route of your workouts.
  • It can find your phone by triggering the ringer of your phone.
  • It receives call and text notifications, so you won’t have to be dependent on your smartphone all the time.
  • It has a bright OLED screen that makes it easy to read data on the screen.
  • It is affordable.

Cons

  • Its remote shutter feature doesn’t work with many phones.
  • The screen has low resolution and limited color.

Final Verdict

It is one of the best-value fitness trackers you will find in the market today. It can be a great companion to your smartphone and help you better understand your health and fitness status. It has a great screen, full GPS and simple interface. It has a glass and metal surface and a silicone strap. At only $70, it is a versatile device that you can wear all the time. It has advanced fitness tracking options that will help you to keep track of your fitness progress. It provides in-depth sleep tracking and so you will be able to see how many hours you have slept and whether the quality of sleep was good or bad.

You can synchronize the band with the Huawei Health app that is available on both iOS and Android. Here you will be able to store your data and look at your previous fitness metrics. If you plan to participate in any marathon or other sports, you will be able to create a training plan with the help of this app.
