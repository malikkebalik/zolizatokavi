Brittany Wagner Bio (Wiki)

Who is Brittany Wagner?

Brittany Wagner is an American TV personality, sports coach, as well as an athletic instructor who rose to fame after appearing in the Netflix documentary series, ‘Last Chance U’.

Brittany Wagner: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

She was born on 15th November 1977 in Clinton, Mississippi, the USA.

She has not provided any information about her parents, siblings, and early childhood. Besides that, she was raised in her hometown Mississippi.

Brittnay holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is unknown.

In which school did Brittany Wagner attend?

She attended the Clinton High School in 1996 and then joined the Mississippi State University in Starkville, Mississippi where she received a bachelor’s degree in Sports Studies in 2000.

She achieved her master’s degree in Sports Administration in 2004.

Brittany Wagner: Professional Life and Career

She started her career in 2002 while working at Jacksonville State University Athletic Department. After that, she accepted a job offer from the University of Mississippi.

She worked at the institution continuously for the next five years.

Then, she was hired as an athletic instructional advisor and compliance assistant at the East Mississippi Community College.

However, she rose to fame in 2016 when she appeared in the widely acclaimed Netflix documentary series, ‘Last Chance U’.

Afterward, she earned her interviews on platforms such as New York Daily News, The Dan Patrick Show, The Kidd Kraddick In the Morning Show, ABC’s Nightline, The New York Times, GQ, and so on.

She made her way to Birmingham, Alabama, in 2017 and began collaborating with the restaurant chain Newk as its marketing director.

Currently, she is working as a motivational speaker, life coach, and athletic academic counselor and has also offered her valuable advice and insights to students in universities and high schools.
