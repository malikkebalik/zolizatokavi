Biography

Ruud Gullit was born in Amsterdam, Netherlands on September 1, 1962. Dutch forward, midfielder and defender who helped AC Milan win three Serie A titles and two European Cups from 1987 to 1993. He was named Dutch Footballer of the Year in 1984 and 1986, won the 1987 Ballon d’Or and was a member of the Dutch national team from 1981 to 1994. He went into TV after retiring, getting to interview notable figures in sports as well as politics, such as Nelson Mandela. He grew up with his family in a single, split-level room in Amsterdam.
