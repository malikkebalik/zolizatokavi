Jackson Mahomes Denies Being Gay and Confirms Attraction to Girls

Jackson Mahome became an internet sensation following his viral Tiktok videos. Fame has also placed the internet personality under a lot of scrutiny, with many people curious about his sexual orientation. Now, some fans want to know, Is Jackson Mahomes gay?

Jackson is the brother of Patrick Mahomes. His brother plays the quarterback position for Kansas City Chiefs and is considered one of the most talented players in the NFL.

While his brother has an established career, Jackson is also successful in his own right. Ever since he began posting videos on the internet, he has garnered over a million followers on Tiktok and thousands of subscribers on other social media platforms, with many keeping tabs on his life.

The controversial internet personality has been making headlines for various reasons. The 22-year-old's sexual orientation is a hot topic of discussion, with many of his trolls labeling him as gay despite setting the record straight about his attraction to girls.

The internet personality opened up about his voice being his biggest insecurity and admitted that his haters have termed him gay judging from how he talks.

While the content creator focuses on putting more videos on the internet, he acknowledged that people calling him gay negatively affects him, noting that he also finds the comments disrespectful to the queer community.

Jackson Mahomes Was Once a Target of Homophobic Slur

Is Jackson Mahomes gay? Some fans were left with unanswered questions after the Tiktok star was verbally abused by a fan at Empower Field at Mile High in Denver. A fan who had gone to watch the Kansas City Chiefs and Denver Broncos was heard verbally abusing the 22-year-old.

In a video shared on Twitter, the unidentified fan can be heard shouting anti-gay slurs at Jackson, who was on the field standing while taking pictures of his brother's wife, Brittany Matthews.

Jackson, who had worn a red Kansas City Chiefs Jacket, didn't respond to the fan, but Matthews was heard shouting back at the fan and pointing out the rude behavior.

Jackson occasionally shares cute photos of them having a good moment together, and they rarely miss watching Patrick's game whenever he plays.

The embarrassing incident occurred a few weeks after Jackson got involved in a public argument in a Kansas City bar after leaving a bad review, citing that the bar didn't accommodate his large group.

The situation got out of hand after Jackson shared a reaction video on social media, lamenting to his thousands of followers about how the waiters were mean and their services were mediocre. The restaurant responded to the matter on Facebook and called Jackson "entitled" but later apologized for how they handled the situation.

The bar released a statement on its Facebook page admitting that its team used social media to personally attack and question someone's character without even knowing him personally. They concluded the report by admitting they were wrong and that the matter could have been handled differently.

Also, in September 2022, Jackson made headlines after he reportedly poured a bottle of water on a Baltimore Ravens fan after his brother's team lost the game.

Jackson Once Revealed He's Not Gay

While many people have been curious to know more about Jackson's sexuality, he set the record straight during a question-and-answer session. In a video uploaded on his Youtube channel, Jackson said he was attracted to girls.

Still, Jackson said he felt bothered when a lot of people asked him if he was gay because of the way he talked. "I think it's actually really a hurtful question because I am friends with a bunch of people that are gay, and It's kinda really disrespectful, in my opinion," he said.
