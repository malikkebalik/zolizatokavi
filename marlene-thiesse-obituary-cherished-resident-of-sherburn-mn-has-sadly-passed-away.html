Marlene Thiesse Obituary, Cherished Resident Of Sherburn, MN Has Sadly Passed Away

Marlene Thiesse Obituary, Death – The community of Sherburn, MN, gathers to commemorate the life of Marlene H. Thiesse, a beloved individual who touched the hearts of many. Born 63 years ago, Marlene’s Celebration of Life Service will take place at 10:00 AM on Wednesday, November 22, 2023, at St. John’s Lutheran Church in Sherburn. This occasion not only honors Marlene’s legacy but also provides solace and support to those who mourn her passing. The service will be a poignant reflection of Marlene’s life, highlighting her contributions, kindness, and the cherished memories she leaves behind.

St. John’s Lutheran Church, a place where Marlene likely found solace and community throughout her life, serves as the fitting backdrop for this commemorative event. The community will come together to share stories, offer condolences, and find comfort in each other’s presence. The significance of Marlene’s life extends beyond the church walls as her final resting place will be at the Sherburn City Cemetery. This sacred ground becomes a symbol of remembrance, a place where friends and family can visit to pay their respects and reflect on the impact she had on their lives.

Prior to the main service, friends and well-wishers will have the opportunity to express their condolences during the visitation hours on Tuesday, November 21, 2023, from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM at St. John’s Lutheran Church in Sherburn. The visitation will also continue for one hour before the service on Wednesday, allowing the community to come together in solidarity and support.

Marlene’s departure on Thursday morning, November 16, 2023, at Methodist Hospital-Mayo Clinic Health Systems in Rochester, MN, has left a void in the hearts of those who knew her. The Lakeview Funeral Home in Fairmont is extending its assistance to the grieving family, ensuring that the arrangements for this solemn occasion are handled with care and compassion. As the community unites to celebrate Marlene’s life, they find strength in shared memories and the enduring impact she had on their lives, ensuring that her spirit lives on in the hearts of all who had the privilege of knowing her.
