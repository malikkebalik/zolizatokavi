Whitney Able Bio (Wiki)

Who is Whitney Able?

Whitney Able is an American actress and model. She is popular for her roles in ‘All the Boys Love Mandy Lane’ and ‘Monsters’.

Also, she has been featured in ‘Maxim’ magazine.

Who are the parents of Whitney Able?

She was born on June 2, 1982, in Houston, Texas, USA. Her birth name is Whitney Nees Able.

Her father’s name is Gary Able and her mother’s name is Linda (priest). She has got two brothers and one of her brothers is Gray. Whitney lived in Spain and Mexico. She is fond of the Spanish language.

Whitney holds American citizenship but their ethnicity is a mix of German, English, French, and Scottish.

Talking about Whitney’s education history, she has not disclosed any information about her institution’s name.

Whitney Able: Professional Life, Career

Whitney Able started her career with the low-budget film ‘Age of Kali’ in 2005. Then, she appeared as Eve in the film ‘Dead Lenny and gained a role in the pilot of the TV show ‘Secrets of a Small Town’ but the show was not picked up by the American Broadcasting Company.

Able made her breakthrough in the horror film ‘All the Boys Love Mandy Lane’ alongside Amber Heard and received mixed reviews from critics. Then, she made her guest appearances in ‘CSI: New York, Cold Case’.

In addition, she made her appearance as Denise in ‘A Walk Among the Tombstones in 2014, Jillian in ‘Ava’s Possessions’ in 2015, and Anna McNue in ‘Godless’ in 2017.

She has been nominated for Seymour Cassel Award in the category Outstanding Performance by an Actress for ‘Dark’ in 2015.
