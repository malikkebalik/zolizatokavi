Boosie Badazz Gifts His Daughter A Pink Mercedes For Her 16th Birthday

Boosie Badazz has gifted his daughter a beautiful pink Mercedes-Benz in celebration of her 16th birthday.

In a video posted to Instagram, Boosie surprised his daughter with the fresh new whip at her 16th birthday party. While there’s no sound, he can be seen approaching his daughter Toriana Hatch with a beaming smile on his face, presenting the keys to her as they hug it out.

AD LOADING...

Boosie then wrote on IG that there was also a bit of spending money in the glove compartment.

@tori_.pressure DADDY LOVE U,” Boosie wrote. “# itoldyalln-ggas N MONEY N THE GLOVE COMPARTMENT. love mines BRAND NEW 2023.”

This is far from the first time Boosie has showered his children with gifts. The “Wipe Me Down” rapper also gifted his daughter Ivi a brand new Porsche Panamera for her 16th birthday back in 2017. He also kickstarted his 2023 by gifting six of his kids custom-made Rolex watches, and in August of 2021 gifted his son $5,000 and presented him with an iced-out diamond chain for his birthday.

In other Boosie news, the 40-year-old rapper revealed that his previously canceled joint album with T.I. is back on, and said they’re also working on a reality show together.
