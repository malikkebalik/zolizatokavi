Nikeet Dhillon Facts

  • She brought to life the character of Bindu in the Punjabi-language action film Sikander 2 in 2019. Her co-stars in the film included Guri, Kartar Cheema, Swan Rupowali, and Shubh Sandhu.
  • Nikeet Dhillon appeared in the 2021 Punjabi-language comedy-drama film Yaar Anmulle Returns, starring Harish Verma, Yuvraj Hans, Prabh Gill, and Navpreet Banga. It is the 3rd film in the Yaar Annmulle film series after Yaar Annmulle and Yaar Annmulle 2.
  • In 2021, she was cast as the female lead in the music video of the song Hawa Aun De by Jigar (ft. Gurlej Akhtar).
  • Along with Guri, Jass Manak, and Priyanka Khera, Nikeet Dhillon co-starred as Jyot in the 2022 Punjabi-language comedy-drama film Jatt Brothers.
