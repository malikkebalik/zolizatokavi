Biography

Catherine O’Hara was born in Toronto, Canada on March 4, 1954. Actress, writer, and comedienne who became known for her roles in Beetlejuice and the Home Alone films. She played Dr. Orwell in Netflix’s A Series of Unfortunate Events. She appeared as Justice Strauss in Lemony Snicket’s A Series of Unfortunate Events, which starred Jim Carrey. She was an understudy for Gilda Radner on Saturday Night Live and was later given an opportunity for a position on the show, but she turned it down and the position was given to her friend Robin Duke.
