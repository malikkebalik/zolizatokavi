Katie Findlays Achievements and Wealth


Katie Findlay, a talented actress of Portuguese, Scottish, and English descent, was born on August 28, 1990, in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. Her acting career has been quite successful, and as of early 2018, her net worth has been estimated at $1 million. Findlay’s wealth has been accumulated through her various roles in the entertainment industry since 2010.

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Katie attended Eric Hamber Secondary School in Vancouver, British Columbia. Before pursuing acting, she trained in ballet for 12 years, but a back injury led her to change paths and delve into the world of acting. Her first roles included appearances in the TV series “Fringe” and the television film “Tangled” in 2010.

Establishing Herself in the Industry

Since then, Katie has landed significant roles, such as Rosie Larsen in the TV series “The Killing”, Maggie Landers in “The Carrie Diaries”, and appearances in films like “The Dark Stranger” and “Jem and the Holograms”. Her recent work includes portraying Lucy in “Man Seeking Woman” and starring in the series “Lost Generation.”

Personal Life and Rumors

When it comes to her personal life, Katie keeps details private. There is little public information regarding her relationships, although there have been rumors linking her to actor Brendan Dooling.
