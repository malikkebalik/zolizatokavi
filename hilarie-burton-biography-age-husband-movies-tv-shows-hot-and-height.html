Hilarie Burton Biography

Hilarie Burton born as Hilarie Ros Burton is an American actress and producer. She is a former host of MTV’s Total Request Live. Burton portrayed Peyton Sawyer on The WB/CW drama One Tree Hill for six seasons (2003–09). She became popular with leading roles in the films Our Very Own, Solstice, and The List.

Burton starred as Sara Ellis on the USA crime drama White Collar from 2010 to 2013. She also had a recurring role as Dr. Lauren Boswell on the ABC medical drama Grey’s Anatomy in 2013. Burton in 2014 she appeared in the short-lived ABC drama series Forever as Molly Dawes, and a recurring role in the short-lived CBS sci-fi drama series Extant as Anna Schaefer in 2015. Burton was cast in a recurring role as DEA Agent Karen Palmer on the Fox television series Lethal Weapon in 2016.

Actress Hilarie Burton

When Burton was working as a VJ for MTV’s Total Request Live (TRL) that when  she got her first big break . Burton was supposed to be a guest commentator for one segment, but producers decided to offer her a permanent job. She continued on to present at the 2000 MTV Video Music Awards and MTV’s Iced Out New Year’s Eve. She also continued on to portray herself on The WB drama Dawson’s Creek in 2002, which was marked as her television debut.

In April 2003 Burton was cast in The WB drama series One Tree Hill.  She potrayed the role of Peyton Sawyer, an independent visual and musical artist and cheerleader. The series premiered on September 23, 2003 and went on to be the network’s top rated program of the year. The role was considered Burton’s breakout role and is her best-known to date. Burton’s role in the series, was featured on the cover of Maxim, American Cheerleader Magazine and People. She received critical praise for her performance  and she earned three Teen Choice Award nominations.  The CW announced in May 2009  that Burton would not be returning for the show’s seventh season based on her own decision not to return, contrary to rumors she left due to salary issues.

Burton made her feature film debut opposite Allison Janney and Cheryl Hines in the drama Our Very Own in June 2005, that centered on five small-town teenagers who dream of a better life and was released to positive reviews from critics. Burton  and her co-stars received the “Outstanding Ensemble Acting” award at the prestigious Sarasota Film Festival in September 2007.  She appeared in the Lifetime drama Normal Adolescent Behavior which followed a group of friends who are in a six-way polyfidelitous relationship. Burton played Ryan. In the same year Burton co-starred in the supernatural horror film Solstice opposite Amanda Seyfried and Elisabeth Harnois.

Burton appeared in the thriller The List as Jo Johnston in June 2008, and was filmed in Wilmington, North Carolina while Burton was on production break for One Tree Hill. The film had a limited release and went on to make $132,863 in the US alone. In the same year, Burton appeared in the Fox Searchlight Pictures drama The Secret Life of Bees as Deborah Owens, the deceased mother of Dakota Fanning’s character.
