  • RYNO Spybots

    • There are 10 RYNO Spybots in Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart. Each of them gives you one blueprint for the RYNO weapon. After you get all of them you can buy the RYNO 8 weapon from Ms. Zurkon. This will unlock "Aim to Misbehave" trophy.

    Index

    All known locations on the map Corson V

    To get here you have to make your way through the factory.

    All known locations on the map Sargasso

    All known locations on the map Scarstu Debris Field

    To get to the platform with the spybot you need to use the Hurlshot to the right of Zurkie's Bar entrance. It will be available after your visit to Torren IV.

    Reward for completing Battleplex Gold Challenge #4 - "Vroom Around".

    All known locations on the map Savali

    On board of a shipwreck you will find a large cave. The entrance to the cave is located near Raritanium.

    All known locations on the map Blizar Prime

    Complete Chef Tulio's side quest and then you will receive a spybot from her.

    All known locations on the map Torren IV

    The bot is located in a cave, which you can descend to via a magnetic walkway located in a side alley.

    All known locations on the map Cordelion

    The spybot is on a platform overhead. Use the rift to get there.

    All known locations on the map Ardolis

    All known locations on the map Viceron
