R. Kellys Alleged Fiance Joycelyn Savage is Releasing a Tell-All Book


Preparation is underway for the release of a tell-all book by R. Kelly’s alleged fiancée and long-time live-in girlfriend, Joycelyn Savage, who is set to shed light on her relationship with the controversial R&B singer.

Confirming the forthcoming publication in an email to The Post, 26-year-old Savage revealed that the book, titled “Love and Joy of Robert”, is expected to be published on Saturday.

When asked about the contents of the book, she mentioned, “All I can say is that it is about Robert, the beginning of my life in Robert’s shadow, where things began to take off, and where they are currently going.”

While remaining tight-lipped about her alleged engagement to Robert Sylvester Kelly, popularly known as R. Kelly, Savage stated, “Everything will be answered in my book,” in response to questions about the status of their relationship.

She did not disclose whether the book would be self-published or released through an established publishing house, or where it can be obtained.

This development follows the revelation of a letter written by Savage to a federal judge, in which she referred to herself as Kelly’s fiancée and appealed for a more lenient sentence for his involvement in decades-long sexual abuse of women and underage individuals. Kelly was eventually sentenced to 30 years in a sex trafficking case.
