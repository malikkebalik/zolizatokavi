Juhi Chawla is a well-reputed actress and beauty queen who started at the age of 19 and has appeared in more than 90 films until 2019. A few of her notable silver screen appearances are in Pratibandh (1990), Bol Radha Bol (1992), Aaina (1993), Hum Hain Rahi Pyar Ke (1993), Darr (1993), Ram Jaane (1995), Deewana Mastana (1997), Yes Boss (1997), Ishq (1997) Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak (1988), Arjun Pandit (1999), Jhankaar Beats (2003), My Brother Nikhil (2005), I Am (2011), Gulaab Gang (2014) and Chalk n Duster (2016). She has also worked with many profound celebrities across the Indian entertainment industry including Amitabh Bachchan, Shahrukh Khan, Aamir Khan, Govinda, Prosenjit Chatterjee, Rishi Kapoor, Anupam Kher, Ajay Devgn, Akshay Kumar, and Sanjay Dutt. On the other hand, Juhi is also the proud receiver of the 1984 “Miss India” title as well as the “Best National Costume Award” at the “Miss Universe” beauty pageant.

With time, Juhi has also won and been nominated for more than 2 dozen awards including reputable ones such as the Filmfare Award, IIFA Award, as Zee Cine Award, and the Bollywood Movie Award. Her achievements over the years have also a garnered Juhi a huge fan base with more than 5 million followers Twitter, more than 2 million followers on Instagram, and more than 2 million followers on Facebook.

    First Film

    Juhi made her debut theatrical film appearance as Zarina in director Mukul S. Anand’s Sultanat in 1964. She worked along with the likes of Dharmendra, Sunny Deol, Sridevi, Amrish Puri, Shakti Kapoor, Tom Alter, and Karan Kapoor.

    She made her first Kannada theatrical film appearance as Shashikala in director Ravichandran’s Premaloka in 1987. The following year the film was released in Tamil under the title, Paruva Ragam. This marked her debut in the Tamil film industry.

    Chawla made her debut Telugu theatrical film appearance as Jaya in Kaliyuga Karnudu in 1988.

    Juhi made her debut Bengali theatrical film appearance as Deepika in director Sujit Guha’s Amar Prem in 1989. She was cast alongside well-known actor and producer Prosenjit Chatterjee.

    She made her first Malayalam theatrical film appearance as Mira Varma in director Aleksa Muhammed Fazil’s Harikrishnans in 1998. Juhi worked along with the likes of distinguished actors Mammotty and Mohanlal.

    Her first Punjabi theatrical film debut was as Jassi in director Manoj Punj’s in Des Hoya Pardes in 2004 alongside well-known singer and actor Gurdas Mann and actress Divya Dutta.

    As a voice actress, she made her debut as the lead character Sita in Ramayana: The Epic in 2010.

    She made her first Gujarati theatrical film appearance as a “Doctor” in director Umang Vyas’s Ventilator in 2018. The film was an adaptation of the 2016 Marathi film with the same title that had been directed by Rajesh Mapuskar and produced by Priyanka Chopra.

    Juhi Chawla Facts

  • She spent the first 4 years of her life alongside her brother Bobby and sister Sonia in Ambala, Haryana. Thereafter, the family briefly lived in Delhi before moving to Mumbai.
  • Juhi’s father served as an Indian Revenue Service officer in the past. Whereas her mother worked in the housekeeping department in The Oberoi, Delhi. It was due to her mother that the family shifted to Mumbai as she had gotten a job a Taj.
  • Before making her way into the film industry in 1986, she competed and was crowned “Miss India” by winning the pageant in 1984. The same year, she also took part in the “Miss Universe” competition and won the title of “Best National Costume.”
  • She was crowned Miss India in 1984 by actress Rekha.
  • She made her debut film appearance in 1986, at the age of 19 alongside many well-known stars like Dharmendra, Sunny Deol, Sridevi, Amrish Puri, and Shakti Kapoor. The film also marked the debut of actor Karan Kapoor.
  • In 1988, Juhi became the first actress to ever win the Filmfare Award for “Lux New Face of the Year.” She received the award for her incredible acting in the Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak in which she acted alongside well-noted actor Aamir Khan.
  • Juhi traveled across the world alongside distinguished celebrities Shahrukh Khan, Akshay Kumar, and Kajol under the title Awesome Foursome in 1998. Amongst the many countries that they traveled to were the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Malaysia.
  • She lost her mother Mona in 1998, who had died in a freak accident while in Prague.
  • She founded the production house Dreamz Unlimited alongside actor Shahrukh Khan and film director Aziz Mirza in the year 2000. The very first film that they launched under their production was the hit Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani (2000). Later, the company’s name was changed to Red Chillies Entertainment. It was headed by Juhi’s brother Bobby until he went into a coma in 2010.
  • Juhi appeared as a judge on the hit reality dance show Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa in 2010.
  • She and her husband Jay partnered with actor Shahrukh Khan in 2008 and bought the T-20 IPL’s Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) for an astonishing $ 75.09 million.
  • In 2009, she was cast in a guest role in American director Steven Speilberg’s romedy film The Hundred Foot Journey alongside actor Om Puri and American actress Helen Mirren.
  • She hosted the Color’s Badmaash Company- Ek Shararat Hone Ko Hai in 2011.
  • Juhi’s brother Bobby, passed away on March 9, 2014. He had been in a coma for almost 4 years after suffering from a heart attack while having dinner at a restaurant in April 2010.
  • Besides just being an amazing actress, Juhi is also a trained classical singer and Kathak dancer.
  • She has worked in films of all languages English, Hindi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada.
  • In 2018, Juhi was chosen as the face of the third edition of the Ministry of Women & Child Development’s “Women’s Organic Festival of India.”
  • Juhi has turned down many roles throughout her career in order to steer clear of the sexy word. Hence, maintaining an elegant and graceful silver screen appearance.
  • She considers herself to be a born traveler and feels most connected to Indonesia and Japan.
  • In 2021, she had filed a lawsuit against the telecom technology up-gradation and 5G trials in High Court which was rejected and called a publicity stunt. She was fined INR 20 lakhs too.
  • Her daughter Jhanvi Mehta is the youngest person to be allowed to sit at the auction table during the IPL auctions in 2019. She was 17 at that time.
  • Juhi lost her sister Sonia in October 2012 to cancer.
  • In the 1990s, actor Salman Khan had offered Juhi’s father to marry her, which was rejected by her father without giving any reason. Later, she married Jay Mehta.
  • In 2023, Juhi planted 1,000 trees on the occasion of the 22nd birthday of her daughter Jahnavi in February. She had planted 500 trees when Jahnavi had turned 21 in February 2022.
