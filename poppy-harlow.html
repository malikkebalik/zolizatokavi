Poppy Harlow

Poppy Harlow is an American journalist who co-anchors “CNN Newsroom” with Jim Sciutto weekdays from 9-11 a.m. ET. She’s also the host of the CNN podcast “BossFiles with Poppy Harlow,” which she created. She previously worked as a corporate analyst for CNN, CNN International, and HLN, as well as an anchor for CNNMoney.com, a reporter, and an editor for Forbes.com Video Network. She also works as a daily relief anchor for CNN’s New Day early-morning news broadcast.

What is Poppy Harlow’s net worth and salary?

Harlow has a net worth of $6 million, which she has accumulated from her career as a writer. Harlow receives a salary that ranges from $40,000 to $ 110,500 per year.

Poppy Harlow Education:

Harlow graduated from Blake Academy, a Minneapolis-based private co-educational preparatory school, in 2001. She went on to Columbia University, where she earned a Phi Beta Kappa Bachelor’s degree in Political Science and Middle Eastern Studies.

Poppy Harlow’s professional life:

Warren Buffett, JPMorgan Chase CEO Jamie Dimon, Google CEO Sundar Pichai, Bill and Melinda Gates, YouTube CEO Susan Wojcicki, Jay-Z, CZI co-founder and co-founder Dr. Priscilla Chan, Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg, former Starbucks CEO Howard Schultz, and Bank of America CEO Brian Moynihan are among the world’s leading industry executives and CEOs questioned by Harlow. She’s conducted interviews with Supreme Court Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg and Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau, as well as moderating two CNN Presidential Town Halls.

She has garnered multiple industry honors, including the Gracie Award for Best Online Financial Crime Investigation Feature and the SABEW Best in Business Web Video Award, and has been nominated for several Emmy awards during her career. Harlow not only co-anchors her show, but she also reports from the field during news breaks. She was assigned to cover the Paris terror attacks in 2015 and stayed for two weeks, focusing her reports on the victims’ and their families’ personal tales.

Harlow anchors comprehensive coverage of the Boston Marathon bombings and reports on the rehabilitation and courage of many ladies who lost limbs in the blast for years afterward. Harlow is particularly interested in economic inequality and ways for closing the gender and minority opportunity gap. She established the multi-media series “American Opportunity,” which examines inequity in the United States.

Harlow joined CNN in 2008 after serving as an anchor for Forbes Video Network and a reporter for NY1 News for a short time. She is a member of the Aspen Institute’s International Affairs Council and the 2019 Henry Crown Fellowship Class of the Aspen Community Leadership Network. Harlow previously served on the Madison Square Boys and Girls Club’s Board of Directors in New York City. Harlow won the John Jay Award for excellent career achievement from Columbia College alumni in 2020.

Poppy Harlow Age:

Harlow will be 38 years old in 2020. She was born in the United States on May 2, 1982, in Saint Paul, Minnesota. Every year on the 2nd of May, she celebrates her birthday.

Poppy Harlow Height:

Harlow stands at 5 feet 5 inches tall (1.65m).

Poppy Harlow Family:

Harlow is the daughter of an attorney, James Lee Harlow, and Mary Louise Baird. Her father died when she was fifteen years old.

Poppy Harlow Husband:

Sinisa Babcic, a finance manager, is Harlow’s wife. The pair married in 2012 and have two daughters, Sienna Babcic, who was born in April 2016 and Luca Babcic, who was born in February 2018.
