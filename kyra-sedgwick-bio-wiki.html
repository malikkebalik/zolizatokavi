Kyra Sedgwick Bio (Wiki)

Who is Kyra Sedgwick?

Kyra Sedgwick is an actress and producer who hails from America.

She gained a lot of popularity and fame for her role as ‘Brenda Leigh Johnson on TNT’s crime drama named The Closer and as ‘Madeline Wuntch’ on the successful sitcom named Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

Moreover, she has earned a Golden Globe Award and an Emmy Award.

Who are the parents of Kyra Sedgwick?

Kyra Sedgwick was born in New York City, New York, US. on August 19, 1965. Kyra Sedgwick is the daughter of Henry Dwight Sedgwick V, a venture capitalist, and Patricia, a speech teacher and educational/family therapist.

She spent her early life and childhood alongside her two brothers named Mike Stern, and Robert Sedgwick, and a sister named Holly Sedgwick.

Kyra Sedgwick is an American by nationality and holds mixed ethnicity of English, Distant Dutch, Scottish, German, and Ashkenazi Jewish.

Education

Reflecting on her education and qualifications, Kyra Sedgwick completed her earlier education at Friends Seminary. Later on, she graduated from the University of Southern California with a degree in theatre studies.

Kyra Sedgwick: Career, Profession

Kyra Sedgwick started her career on the television soap opera named Another World at the age of 16. After that, she appeared in several Hollywood movies such as Singles, Heart and Souls, Something to Talk About, and Phenomenon.

Kyra Sedgwick starred as a Jewish immigrant in a popular TV film named Miss Rose White. She acted as Mae Coleman in the movie Secondhand Lions in 2004 and as Stella Peck in The Game Plan in the year 2007.

Also, she starred with her husband Kevin Bacon in the film named The Woodsman in 2004.

Later on, she starred as Deputy Chief Brenda Leigh Johnson in the television series named The Closer starting from 2005 to 2012.

For her role in the series, she garnered a Golden Globe award in 2007 for her performance as a lead actress following a Primetime Emmy Award in the year 2010. In 2009, she was honored with a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for Television.

Kyra Sedgwick produced the television series called Proof for TNT which premiered in 2015 for one season.

As a result of a successful career for such a long time, has presented her fame as well as fortune.
