Former Police Officer Joseph Foti from East Haven dies at Griffin Hospital

Shelton, CT – The community of Shelton mourns the unexpected loss of Joseph Foti, who passed away suddenly on Thursday, December 8, 2023, leaving a profound impact on many lives.

Described as someone who touched hearts during his lifetime, Joseph Foti’s sudden demise has left a void in the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him. Since the news broke, an outpouring of heartfelt condolences has been shared with Foti’s family, accompanied by tributes honoring his life.

Joseph Foti, 54, passed away at Griffin Hospital in Shelton on Monday, December 4, 2023. Born in New Haven on March 26th, Foti served as a dedicated police officer in New Haven before retiring.

Janine Caputo – Foti posted on Facebook,

“To my son Joseph Thomas Foti, we had an unimaginable year! But your strength,kindness,and love has gotten us through. But today is about you!! So I wish for you nothing but happiness and to celebrate you at 23!!!!!your amazing human I’m so proud of the hard working man you have become I love you always! Enjoy your day”

Survived by his children, Samuel and Ethan Foti, along with his brothers, former spouse Leslie Foti, and nieces and nephews, Joseph Foti leaves behind cherished memories and a legacy of service.

The circumstances and the precise cause of Foti’s passing remain undisclosed at this time. The details surrounding his sudden death are yet to be revealed, awaiting further updates from official sources.

The family of Joseph Foti is expected to release information regarding his obituary and funeral arrangements in the coming days.
