In another conversation during the stream, Kai Cenat, Druski, and another inmate discussed life outside jail.

Druski asked, “What do you miss about the free world, bro?” To this Cenat responded, “What do you mean about the free world? I just miss the typical stuff. I used to have a routine, and I miss being with my family, my baby mama, really everybody. I truly understand that.”

The other inmate sitting with Druski and Cenat added, “I feel you, It's just not the same. Yeah. Besides that, I'm fine. This type of thing is cool though. I'm in here with the game. We all have recreation, so it's not that bad. Don't just sit around here.”
