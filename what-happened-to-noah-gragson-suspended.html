What happened to Noah Gragson? Suspended

What happened to Noah Gragson? Suspended – American stock car racer Noah Gragson is a talented driver who has established himself in the NASCAR circuit. He has participated in a number of series, displaying his prowess and love for the game. In the NASCAR Cup Series, Gragson has competed on a regular basis with Legacy Motor Club, piloting the No. 42 Chevrolet Camaro ZL1.

He also had successful runs in the NASCAR Xfinity Series from 2019 to 2022, driving for JR Motorsports, and in the NASCAR Camping World Truck Series from 2017 to 2018, driving for Kyle Busch Motorsports. Gragson has established himself as a leading figure in the racing industry because to his hard work and accomplishments.

What happened to Noah Gragson?

Due to his behavior on social media, Noah Gragson, a NASCAR driver for Legacy Motor Club, has been banned from competition indefinitely. He was suspended after like a contentious meme that ridiculed the tragic death of George Floyd, an event that ignited heated discussions on race all across the world.

Gragson was immediately suspended by Legacy Motor Club after they posted a statement on social media indicating that his actions were against the team’s principles. Gragson won’t be competing in the upcoming NASCAR Cup Series race at Michigan International Speedway as a result of the ban; Josh Berry will take his place.

Why he got suspended?

After liking a social media meme that mocked George Floyd’s death, Noah Gragson was threatened with expulsion from Legacy Motor Club and NASCAR. The meme made fun of the terrible incident in 2020 when a white police officer knelt on a Black man’s neck for more than nine minutes, causing George Floyd to die.

The Legacy Motor Club team swiftly decided to suspend Gragson indefinitely because the post’s crudeness and offensiveness went against the principles they uphold. Additionally commenting on the situation, NASCAR claimed that Gragson’s actions violated the Member Conduct part of their rulebook, supporting the suspension decision.

What meme favored by Noah Gragson?

The reason for Noah Gragson’s suspension was that he approved of a social media meme mocking George Floyd’s passing. The earlier-in-the-year meme made light of the terrible circumstances surrounding George Floyd’s death by portraying his visage in an offensive and contemptuous way.

Given the sensitivity of the occurrence and its significance in igniting discussions about racial injustice all around the world, the contentious post generated a stir and was regarded highly inappropriate. The NASCAR Rule Book’s Member Conduct section and Legacy Motor Club’s ideals were both violated by Gragson’s love of the meme, which led to his ban from both organizations.
