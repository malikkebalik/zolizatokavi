How Martin Lawrence Celebrated His Fiance Roberta Moradfar's Birthday

Martin Lawrence wishes his fiancée a happy birthday on Instagram with a slideshow of photos celebrating their love.

The “Bad Boys” actor took to his Instagram to shower his long-time partner, Roberta Moradfar with love in celebration of her birthday.

Roberta Moradfar and Martin Lawrence at the 47th AFI Life Achievement Award at Dolby Theatre on June 06, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

In his caption, he described her as “beautiful,” “wonderful,” and “lovely," and rounded it off by calling her “the best.”

Although [Matin Lawrence] is a successful actor and comedian, [Roberta Moradfar] is a businesswoman with a cosmetic medicine career spanning over eight years.

Martin’s selection of photos showed how goofy and in love they are and some fans took note of this with one writing,“Awww happy birthday hun! @martinlawrence so glad you’re happy.”

In 2018, the actor also showed off his humorous side when he paid Roberta a tribute on her birthday, in a now-deleted post on Instagram.

According to Essence, he jokingly shared some of the things he loves about her with an honorable mention of her pancake-making skills, which he praised.

The outlet recalls that the couple got engaged in April 2017, adding that Lawrence has been married twice before. Firstly to Patricia Southall and then to Shamika Gibbs, from which he has three daughters.

However, as reported by Black Doctor, the father-of-three spared no expense in choosing a perfect ring for his bride-to-be.

With information from a source, the news outlet shared that the “Big Momma’s House” actor worked with Iconic Jewels Beverly Hills for months to create her custom 5-carat emerald cut engagement ring with the couple’s initials spelled out in diamonds on the band.

Late last year, the duo made headlines after they were seen feeding acting cozy during an NBA game between LA Clippers and Utah Jazz.

Daily Mail reports that Roberta, an aesthetics nurse practitioner, was caught on camera feeding her man, while they watched the game courtside.

The NBA appearance came weeks after Lawrence and his “Bad Boys” cast-mate, Will Smith, announced that the long-awaited “Bad Boys” sequel would be premiering soon.

People magazine shares that the following year, Moradfar was at the movie premiere with his three daughters to support her man, and the pair wore matching outfits.

Cheat Sheet notes that although her fiancé is a successful actor and comedian, the birthday celebrant is a businesswoman with a cosmetic medicine career spanning over eight years.
