Indiana Evans is an Australian actor, singer, and songwriter who is known for playing Matilda Hunter in the soap opera Home and Away, Bella in the fantasy teen drama series H2O: Just Add Water, Tatum Novak in the drama series Crownies, Natalie Crawford in the mystery anthology series Secrets and Lies, Abby Oakley in the children’s television series Snobs, Melissa in the comedy horror show Ash vs Evil Dead, and Tash in the comedy-drama series House Husbands. In addition to that, she has also been cast in movies like A Model Daughter: The Killing of Caroline Byrne, At the Tattooist, Smith, Arctic Blast, Gingers, Blue Lagoon: The Awakening, and Burden.
