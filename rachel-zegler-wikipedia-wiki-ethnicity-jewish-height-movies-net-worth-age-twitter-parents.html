Rachel Zegler Bio

For her role, she was honoured with the Golden Globe for Best Actress – Motion Picture Comedy or Musical. In the superhero movie Shazam! Fury of the Gods (2023), she later portrayed Anthea.

Zegler was conceived by a Colombian mother and a Polish father in Hackensack, New Jersey. She started out in musical theatre, appearing in 42nd Street, The Little Mermaid, and Beauty and the Beast. She shared a rendition of Lady Gaga’s song “Shallow” on YouTube in 2018, which attracted the attention of West Side Story casting directors. After receiving over 30,000 applications for an open casting call, she was chosen to play Mara, the female lead.

Zegler’s performance in West Side Story received excellent reviews. She received compliments on her acting, dancing, and singing.
