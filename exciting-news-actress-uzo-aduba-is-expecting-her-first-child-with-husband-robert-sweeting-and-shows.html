Exciting News: Actress Uzo Aduba is Expecting her First Child with Husband Robert Sweeting and Shows



 

 

Uzo Aduba, the Nigerian-American actress, has announced that she and her husband Robert Sweeting are expecting their first child.

 

The 42-year-old shared the happy news on the red carpet of the 2023 Tony Awards on Sunday, June 11. 

Aduba, who was wearing an orange Christian Siriano outfit, revealed her baby bump by unbuttoning her coat, confirming the news to People during the Tony Awards red carpet event. A representative also confirmed her pregnancy.

‘The happy couple is over the moon about their growing family and are excited to be parents,’ said an unnamed insider.

She proudly displayed her baby bump in the oversized matching orange pants while on the red carpet.

 

The Orange is the New Black star accessorized with David Webb jewelry, Alexandre Birman shoes, and a Judith Leiber clutch. 

 

She later shared her joy on Instagram, writing, ‘What a blessing. My husband Robert and I are so happy to enter this next chapter together as parents. I am beyond excited. I get to be someone’s Mommy! I know as the three of us continue to grow, our only, ONLY goal will be to love you, little one. My goodness. Look what God has done.’

 

It was confirmed in September 2021 that Aduba and Robert Sweeting had quietly tied the knot in a secret New York ceremony in 2020.
