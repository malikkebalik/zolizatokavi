Southern Cayuga High School graduate Ana Marsh passes away in car accident

Cayuga County, NY – The Southern Cayuga community is in mourning as it grapples with the untimely passing of Ana Marsh, a cherished graduate of Southern Cayuga High School. Ana, surrounded by her loving family, tragically lost her life on Monday, December 11, 2023, succumbing to injuries sustained in a car accident that occurred just days before.

The devastating news was confirmed through a heart-wrenching publication at 1:38 pm on December 11, expressing the profound grief of those close to her. The message read, “I don’t know how to do this life without Ana. Beyond devastated. I thought we had her for my lifetime, but we only had her for hers. Daryl and I both woke up at 4:07 am with a start but with no clear reason.”

Ana Marsh, described as a beacon of positivity and genuine warmth, touched the lives of many with her straightforward demeanor. Her humility and authenticity shone through in every activity she pursued, making her not only a favorite among classmates but also among her teachers.

Known for her empathy and compassion, Ana’s commitment to her studies and insatiable thirst for knowledge earned her the respect and admiration of her peers and educators alike. Her sense of humor and welcoming personality made her a beloved figure, and her absence will be deeply felt by all who knew her.

In response to the tragic incident, a GoFundMe account was established on December 10, 2023, by Erin Tones and two others to support Ana’s family. The campaign has garnered overwhelming support, with a total of $16,347 raised from 236 donations, the largest being $500. The fundraiser aims to reach a goal of $40,000 to assist Ana’s family with medical expenses and related costs.

A statement from the GoFundMe account shared details of the accident, stating, “On December 7, Ana was in a horrific car accident near her home in Cayuga County, New York. She was air-lifted to the trauma center at Strong Memorial Hospital in Rochester, where she continues to receive treatment with her loving family by her side.”

As the community mourns the loss of Ana Marsh, they come together to support her family during this challenging time, remembering her as a radiant spirit that will be dearly missed.
