Avika Gor Wiki, Biography, Age, Movies, TV Shows, Family, Images

Avika Gor is an Indian actress and model who mostly works in Bollywood films and television series. She made her television debut through the Balika Vadhu serial, in which she featured in the role of Anandi Khajaan Singh/Anandi Jagdish Singh. Then she made her debut movie Morning Walk as Garigi. She was a contestant in Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi 9 which get telecasted on Colors TV. Her performance in the 1920 Horrors of the Heart received critical acclaim.
