Who Is Negan From Walking Dead?

Negan is a fictional character from the comic series ‘The Walking Dead,’ which also appears in the TV series, in name Negan Smith. He was the leader of a survivor group called Saviors. The group oppresses the other survivors and forces them to pay tributes, in exchange for production against walkers. The character was played by Jeffrey Dean Morgan. His first appearance was in the episode ‘Last Day on Earth’, and his final appearance was in ‘Rest in Peace.’

What Happened To Negan In The Walking Dead?

The character of Negan first appeared in season 6 of the Walking Dead as a villainous character. He killed many people in the series, and he was afraid that their relatives or friend would take revenge. In the following season, he was changed, and he tried to redeem himself from his past actions. Negan is happy that his wife Annie is expecting a child and wants to build a family with them. At the season finale of the 11th season, Negan joins the soldiers to save the Commonwealth alongside Maggie. After destroying all the walkers, he and Maggie would have a heartfelt conversation with each other. He seeks forgiveness for killing Glenn. But Maggie says that she won’t forgive him and she will try to move on. At last Negan and Annie leave the Commonwealth to start a new life.

Who Kills Negan In The Walking Dead?

In season 8, Rick slits Negan’s throat with a glass. Negan falls down and struggles for life. Rick gives a thought about it and tells Siddiq to save him. He proclaims that Negan’s rule is over and asks the saviors to unite and fight against the walkers. After recovering, he was sentenced in a prison for the rest of his life. However, he escaped from that prison later.

When Does Negan Die In The Walking Dead Show?

Negan is not dead in the series. However, in the final episode of season 8 called ‘Wrath’, he had a near-death experience as his throat was cut by Rick. He was saved from death by Siddiq, who was asked to do so by Rick.
