What Is Sheba Turk, WWL Reporter's Age &amp; Who Is Boyfriend, Revealed

Sheba Turk is an American journalist, speaker, and author. She is most widely known as the weekday morning anchor and reporter for WWL-TV’s (CBS tv station based in New Orleans) Eyewitness Morning News.

She was a former host for the show The 504 which came to an end earlier in 2018. Besides being a journalist, she is also an author who penned the book Off Air: My Journey To The Anchor Desk (2018).
