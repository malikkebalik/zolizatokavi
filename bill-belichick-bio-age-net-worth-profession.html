Bill Belichick Bio, Age, Net Worth, Profession

Where was Bill Belichick born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

On April 16, 1952, Bill Belichick was born in Nashville, Tennessee, to Jeannette (Munn) and Steve Belichick. Bill was given his name in honor of Bill Edwards, a legendary coach in college football.

Belichick is of Croatian ancestry; in 1897, his paternal grandparents, Ivan Bilii and Marija (Mary) Barkovi, left the Croatian village of Dragani, Karlovac, and moved to Monessen, Pennsylvania. He was raised in Annapolis, Maryland, where his dad served as the US Naval Academy’s assistant football coach.
