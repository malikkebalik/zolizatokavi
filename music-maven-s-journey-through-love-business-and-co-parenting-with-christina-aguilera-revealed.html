Christina Aguilera And Jordan Bratman Relationship Timeline

Aguilera and Bratman were both in love with each other. The Butterfly singer said that her marriage with the music executive felt right.

Aguilera's Infedility & Separation With Jordan 

As mentioned, Aguilera and Bratman were fully in love with each other. Even so, the former pair separated after five years of marriage. Turns out, the I Turn to You singer had an extramarital affair with fellow singer Matthew Rutler.

The divorce was finalized in April 2011. The music executive Bratman and his former wife kept the divorce settlement private. However, it is known that they have joint custody of their son.

Jordan Bratman's Early Life

Bratman was born in South Bronx, New York on June 4, 1977. He grew up in an artistic family to Gail and Jack Bratman. Jordan's father, Jack, was a renowned music producer himself.

Encouraged and inspired by his father's success, Jordan also practiced music at a young age. At the tender age of 16, he started a music career and found initial success.

The 5 ft 9 inches tall Jordan pursued a Business of Arts degree in management at the Tulane University. At that time, he was working at the American Sector record label and worked with several emerging artists.
