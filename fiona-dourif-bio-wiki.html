Who is Fiona Dourif?

Fiona Dourif: Professional Life and Career

Fiona Dourif debuted her acting career at the age of 23 after portraying the role of Chez Ami Whore in the television series ‘Deadwood’.

By the following year, she appeared as Alice in the television mini-series ‘Theif’ and made her film debut in ‘Little Chenier’.

Fiona Dourif appeared as Susan in the short film ‘The Lucky One in 2007. After a year, she portrayed the character Becky in the indie film ‘Garden Party and starred as Detective Nikki Breslin on ‘Law & Order: Special Victims Unit’.

Fiona Dourif’s other notable performances include ‘The Messanger’ in 2009, ‘’Letters from the Big Man’ in 2011, ‘True Blood’ in 2011, ‘The Master’ in 2012, ‘Gutshot Straight’ in 2014, etc.

She played the character Rachel Griffiths in the ABC mini-series ‘When We Rise’ in 2016 and played Brie in the indie thriller ‘Blood is Blood’.

Recently, she appeared as Jennifer Reddington in ‘The Blacklist and as Good Leader Tavis in ‘The Purge in 2018.
