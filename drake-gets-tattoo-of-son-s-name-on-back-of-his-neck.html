Drake Gets Tattoo Of Sons Name On Back Of His Neck

Drake has gone from what Pusha T once called “hiding a child” to now baring his son’s name on his neck loudly and proudly for the world to see.

On Monday (August 7), the OVO rapper was spotted with “ADONIS” written in all capitals on the back of his neck – adding to what was already a pretty large tattoo collection. Per Pop Sugar‘s last count in May, Drizzy was already passed 55 tats, including his mom’s initials on his face that he got last summer, a portrait of his father on his arm, a tribute to the late Virgil Abloh and many more.

AD LOADING...

This isn’t the first tat dedicated to his five-year-old either, as Drizzy debuted portraits of Adonis, producer Noah “40” Shebib, Aaliyah, Denzel Washington and Lil Wayne across his back in summer 2020.

Back in February, the 6 God and his son Adonis were the special guests on an episode of Sundae Conversation with Barstool Sports’ Caleb Pressley. The conversation was full of hilarious and heartwarming moments – including Drizzy speaking on being a dad and Adonis shedding light on his father’s parenting skills.

The five-year-old opened the interview by speaking about his friends before switching the topic to his dad, who was sitting beside him.

After Adonis admitted he’s not the strongest reader, Pressley asked Adonis: “Do you think if he did a better job with you at home that you’d be able to read?” To which the curly-haired kid jokingly replied: “Yeah.”

However, Adonis then saluted Drake for his sense of humor, calling him “a funny dad” and saying that he “does a lot of funny jokes.”

AD LOADING...

Pressley then began interviewing Drake and asked him hilarious questions such as if Adonis has ever “cock-blocked” him and if he walks around his Air Drake plane in just his boxers.

The topic of betting also came up, and Pressley asked Drizzy what he thinks is the best thing to put his chips on, and he wasted no time saying fatherhood is the way to go.

AD LOADING...

“Fatherhood,” Drake replied. “I just mean, in an encouraging way, like, ‘You can always bet on fatherhood. For all the people out there, you can always bet on fatherhood.”

Adonis closed out the interview with a funny moment where he was gifted a Kawhi Leonard Los Angeles Clippers jersey. Pressley encouraged Adonis to say thank you, but the 6 God’s seed ignored him and instead said his favorite number is two.

That led Pressley to ask if Adonis likes to “go number two,” to which he unknowingly replied: “Yeah” before saying “Bye-bye” and that he is “a shooting star.”
